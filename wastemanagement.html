How We Process Collected Waste

Garbage Waste Processing

  • Waste transported to sorting facility.
  • Separated into dry, wet, and hazardous waste.
  • Wet waste used for compost and biogas.
  • Dry waste sent for recycling.
  • Remaining waste disposed safely.

Waste Clothes Processing

  • Clothes sorted by condition.
  • Reusable clothes donated to NGOs.
  • Damaged fabric sent to recycling units.
  • Reusable material used for industrial purposes.
